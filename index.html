Rainbow Buddha Bowl

Colourful · Nourishing · Ready in 20 min · Totally Instagrammable

🛒 Ingredients (serves 2)
🍚 1 cup brown rice 🥑 1 avocado 🥕 2 carrots 🫘 1 can chickpeas 🥒 ½ cucumber 🌽 ½ cup sweetcorn 🥬 2 handfuls spinach 🍋 1 lemon 🧄 2 garlic cloves 2 tbsp tahini 1 tbsp olive oil soy sauce · paprika · salt
👩‍🍳 Steps
  1. 1
    Cook the rice according to packet instructions. While it cooks, preheat your oven to 200 °C (400 °F).
  2. 2
    Drain & rinse chickpeas. Toss with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder and a pinch of salt. Roast on a baking tray for 20 min until crispy.
  3. 3
    Peel & ribbon or grate the carrots. Slice the cucumber into half-moons. Halve and slice the avocado. Keep everything separate — the rainbow effect is the whole vibe!
  4. 4
    Make the tahini dressing: whisk 2 tbsp tahini + juice of ½ lemon + 1 crushed garlic clove + 1 tbsp soy sauce + 3 tbsp water until smooth and pourable.
  5. 5
    Divide rice between two bowls. Arrange spinach, carrots, cucumber, sweetcorn, avocado and crispy chickpeas in colourful sections on top.
  6. 6
    Drizzle tahini dressing generously over everything. Finish with a squeeze of lemon and sesame seeds if you have them. Enjoy! 🌸
💡 Pro tip: Batch-roast a big tray of chickpeas on Sunday and use them all week as a protein-packed snack or salad topper!
